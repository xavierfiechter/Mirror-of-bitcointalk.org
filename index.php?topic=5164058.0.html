1. THE BOUNTY CAMPAIGN WILL RUN UNTIL THE TEAM DECIDES TO STOP IT

You must be prepared that any work you perform will not be paid. You cannot make any claims. If these rules do not suit you, please refuse any type of participation in our bounty campaign.

6. The team retains the right to modify the campaign rules. Follow the updates in this thread.

7. Newbie accounts can participate in content and video campaigns.
