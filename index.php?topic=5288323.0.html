🔮 Howdy, Forecasters! 🔮

We’re delighted to announce that Futuur, the world’s leading play-money prediction market, is now offering real-money markets in Bitcoin BTC!

Futuur is a prediction market, a people-powered forecasting engine where you can bet on real-world events, from sports to politics to science and everything in between. Our mission is to help improve decision-making by generating more accurate predictions about the greatest possible range of future events.  We do this by aggregating the “wisdom of the crowd” into a single, easy to understand percentage estimate for the likelihood of that event to occur.





Offering more than a thousand active markets! Check out current forecasts in:

💼  Business 💼
👏  Entertainment 👏
⚽  Sports ⚽
🗳️  Politics 🗳️
🔬  Science 🔬


It’s totally free to join and bet as much as you want using Ooms, our play-money currency! We’ve done our best to make the interface fast, fun, and social. Comment on markets, follow your friends to find out what they’re forecasting, and make your way up our leaderboards!

We have been operating play-money markets since 2017, and to date have resolved more than 35,000 markets with bets from over 15,000 forecasters. And we’ve seen a lot of success! Our average Brier score (the measurement statisticians use to evaluate the accuracy of probabilistic predictions) of 0.163 is a strong confirmation that our crowd-powered predictions actually work!

And now, you can bet with Bitcoin!  Bitcoin markets will operate in parallel to our Ooms markets, and will generate separate forecasts.  We think that this additional financial incentive -- more “skin in the game” -- will bring even more accurate forecasts as a result!

hi TimeTeller!  Yes, our original thread was moved to the Games and Rounds forum, so we removed the announcement part and posted here.  

Regarding longer-term bets: one thing to note is that you can sell a bet at any time, so if you believe that your longer term prediction is right, and that other people will bet on that outcome as new information becomes available, then you can expect the price to go up.

Prices on Futuur are determined by market activity, similar to a stock exchange.  This means that if other people bet on an outcome you already bet one, its price will rise and you will be able to sell for a profit.  

That said, we also have lots of shorter term markets, and are always open to suggestions for new markets!

As it turns out, gambling and predicting the future have a lot to do with one another, at least when it comes to prediction markets like Futuur!     

Prediction markets provide an incentive for bettors like you to contribute the information they have regarding an event, and provide a mechanism to aggregate everybody's information into a single probabilistic estimate.

To take your example, let's say that there's an asteroid that seems to be coming in the direction of earth.  We could offer a market for it, and this would provide an incentive for people with information regarding the event (for instance, a student of astrophysics who has been studying this asteroid) to make a bet on the outcome.  As more people do this, the price of that outcome will change, and tend towards its actual chance of occurring. 

There has been a good amount of research done on this, and in general the result is that predictions generated through a prediction market do better, on average, than other methods.

• wikipedia article on prediction markets: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prediction_market
