The development of digital technology (Cryptocurrency) or more specifically (Bitcoin) is increasingly fast and has a significant effect on all dimensions of human life, one of which is in the financial sector. The presence of Bitcoin using blockchain technology has changed the financial system that has been used and of course gave birth to pros and cons. There are several countries that make Bitcoin a legal tender and there are also those who reject the presence of Bitcoin in their country. But despite all the pros and cons, the interest of people around the world in Bitcoin is increasing because it is resistant to inflation.

Educating ordinary people to be more familiar with cryptocurrencies, especially Bitcoin can increase people's interest in using crypto, they will not hesitate to make Bitcoin a long-term investment asset because its value is increasing from year to year. Another reason that drives people's interest in Bitcoin is because it was initially introduced to the public without value, now Bitcoin has a fantastic value and is the target of many investors.
