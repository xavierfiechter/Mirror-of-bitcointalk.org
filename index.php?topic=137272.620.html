This is where the public place comes in to play. Not only does it make sure you won't get robbed at gunpoint, but you can also get help fast if needed. If you have to, then have the person meet you in a police station or some public place within a walking distance of one. This will insure that the police can arrive there as quick as possible if needed. If you're selling a very large amount, carry a taser. If he starts to run away, hit him with the taser and call the police. I'd be more worried about someone trying to rob me at gunpoint than I would be about them running away right after sending the funds. You could catch them rather easily. If they get into a car, get the license plate number.

When you are trying to have a transaction to the other people you met through online better while you are having a conversation to them get some validation of information as a proven to have a legitimate transaction most of the common problem today is no information of the other party wants to transact if you don't want better to make a meetup transaction to verify the items or get some midman to handle both things and make sure the security of the traders.

Actually there is 3 kinds of method for transaction or to cash your coins.
1. Meeting
If you are transferring a big amount you should do this by helding a meeting with the buyer.
It is the safest transaction method. You can easily transfer your amount while the person is in front of you. There is no risk in this method.

2. Transfer in Portion
If your amount is not so big to held o meet up or not so small to transfer all together you should follow this method.
In this method you have to divide your amount in 2/3 parts. Then u have to send the first portion and have to wait for the seller to send your amount.
Then you just have to repeat the method.

3. All in
If your amount is too small. You should better to transfer your all amount despite using the above methods. For small amount u cant afford the cost of those methods.
But make sure that the seller worth your trust.
