NASTYFANS INTRODUCTION:
NastyFans is a club for Bitcoin Enthusiasts established in 2012 to show how a fun & transparent community project can be built with Bitcoin.  Using the auction at nastyfans.org to trade for a seat entitles you to a vote in club polls and a portion of NastyFans Bitcoin donation distributions.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:

HOW CAN I JOIN NASTYFANS?
Bitcointalk user nonnakip has started a website for NastyFans where members can trade seats using an auction.

CAN I HELP SPREAD THE WORD?
Sure.  Check out the signature campaign thread.

WHERE DO DONATIONS COME FROM?
Members are encouraged to develop new innovative use cases for Bitcoin and support each other's efforts as well as give back to NastyFans in the form of donations.  The NastyMining Green Energy Project is one such development that donates all of it's hashrate to mining on behalf of the NastyFans Donation Address.  The NastyShop is another example where a portion of trades for goods is donated to NastyFans.  You can view the history of all donation distributions here: Historic Distribution List.

When people expand they're supposed to, at some point, be able to do it out of their company's profits. Ultimately a company which must return to the market time and time again and never provides any accretive value for shareholders, is a very poorly run company. The idea of "mining bonds" has to be revisited here. These are not bonds, they're shares with a fixed dividend. A bond is a paper security which represents a loan. Once people begin to wake up and smell the toast burning, I personally feel the current concept of "mining bonds" will disappear in a puff of smoke.
